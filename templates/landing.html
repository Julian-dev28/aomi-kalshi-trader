How It Works

Six steps.
One decision.

From analysis trigger to executed order in a single agent cycle. OpenTrader queries Hyperliquid, searches the web, reasons, and executes — or hands you the trade button.

01 Position & Orders get_clearinghouse_state reads your live equity, open position, and unrealized PnL. get_open_orders catches any stale orders before placing new ones.
02 Live Price & Book get_all_mids pulls the live BTC mid price. get_l2_book snapshots full order book depth — bid and ask pressure at every level.
03 Candle Momentum get_candle_snapshot pulls the last 10 15-minute candles. Direction, acceleration, and structure — the primary signal driving every verdict.
04 Funding & Fills get_funding_history checks the current perpetual funding rate — positive means longs are paying. get_user_fills shows the last 5 executions for context.
05 AI Verdict Agent synthesizes all 7 data sources and streams a direct verdict: LONG / SHORT / CLOSE / PASS — with confidence %. 60%+ is enough to act.
06 Risk Gate & Execute Kelly criterion sizes the trade from live equity. 5× leverage. Auto Mode places the IOC order on Hyperliquid. Real orders, real settlement, zero clicks.

OpenTrader Hyperliquid Tools

8 live data tools.
Called every cycle.

Every agent cycle queries all 8 Hyperliquid tools before deciding. No stale data, no guesses — every verdict is grounded in the current state of the market and your account.

get_clearinghouse_state Position & Equity Live account equity, open position side/size, entry price, unrealized PnL, and margin usage — checked first every cycle.
get_open_orders Open Orders All pending open orders for the account — ensures stale orders are caught before a new one is placed.
get_all_mids Live Mid Prices Current mid prices for all listed assets. Used to anchor the BTC price snapshot at decision time.
get_l2_book Order Book Depth Full L2 order book — bid and ask sizes at every price level. Reveals real buying and selling pressure.
get_candle_snapshot Candlestick Data OHLCV candles at 15m and 1h intervals. Direction and acceleration of the last 10 candles drive the primary verdict signal.
get_funding_history Funding Rate Current perpetual funding rate. Positive = longs pay shorts, a cost that erodes hold time. Factored into LONG conviction.
get_user_fills Trade History Last 5 fills for the account — recent execution context so the agent knows what was just traded and at what price.
get_meta Exchange Metadata Asset specs, size decimals, tick sizes, and universe config from Hyperliquid — available on demand for order validation.
